Introduction

This web page is to provide different information about the two entities of PIMS, medical products and the employees. You have to create an account to access this information. Once you login, you will be able to see details of PIMS.

Scope

This project has a very wide scope as information related to medicine and similar products is always needed at pharmaceutical companies. If it is computerized, it becomes easier to access, insert, delete or update the data and make it available to all users. A single database is more cost and space effective because it does not take up any filing (hard copy) space and maintaining it is cheap. In short, there is a market available for this product.

Functionalities

The project will include following features:
1. It stores all the relevant information of medicines (for example ingredients, dosage, price etc.).
2. Staff salary sheet is generated where bonuses and deductions are also maintained.
3. Information related to the distributers is included.
4. You can search for products through product or company name.
5. You can insert, delete and update the data. 6. The database is updated according to insertion or deletion of any data.
7. Constraints are applied.
8. Side functionality: Patients and doctors might be able to view this database according to their needs.
